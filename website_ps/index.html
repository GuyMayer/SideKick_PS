Frequently Asked Questions

Which versions of ProSelect does it support?
ProSelect 2022, 2024, and 2025 are all supported. SideKick auto-detects which version you're running.
Do I need a GoHighLevel account?
For invoice sync and client import, yes ‚Äî you need a GHL account with API access. The payment plan calculator and room capture work without GHL.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. All data flows directly between your computer and GoHighLevel's API. Nothing is stored on our servers. Your API keys are encrypted and saved locally on your machine only.
What if I switch computers?
Export your settings from the old computer and import them on the new one. Your license is tied to your GHL Location ID, not a specific machine ‚Äî just deactivate on the old computer and activate on the new one.
Does it work with GoCardless / Direct Debit?
Yes. The payment calculator supports GoCardless Direct Debit as a payment method and automatically enforces the 4-business-day setup window that DD requires. You can mix payment methods ‚Äî for example, a credit card deposit with DD recurring payments.
