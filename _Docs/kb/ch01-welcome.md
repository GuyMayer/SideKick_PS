## Why this matters

You became a photographer to create beautiful images — not to spend your
evenings typing invoices, chasing missed payments, or copying client details
between programmes. SideKick PS handles all of that for you. It sits quietly
alongside ProSelect, and with one click on the toolbar it syncs your order to
GoHighLevel, builds a payment plan, sets up a Direct Debit, or sends a
printed thank-you card to your client's doormat.

By the end of today, you will have:

- Fewer clients who "forget" to pay
- Invoices that create themselves
- A thank-you card landing on their mat before the excitement fades
- Your evenings back

## What SideKick PS actually does

SideKick PS is a floating toolbar that docks to your ProSelect window. Each
button does one thing — and each thing saves you about five minutes of typing
per client. Over a year, that is dozens of hours you get back.

Here is what each section of the toolbar does for you:

| Section | What it handles |
|---------|----------------|
| **GHL** (left) | Look up clients, sync invoices, open their GoHighLevel record |
| **Shortcuts** (middle) | Review orders, capture room photos, refresh albums, quick print, PDFs, QR codes |
| **Services** (right) | GoCardless Direct Debit mandates, Cardly printed postcards |

It is not a separate app you have to learn. It lives inside ProSelect. You
click a button. It does the thing. You carry on with your sale.

This is not a technical reference. It is written for a busy photographer who

  arrival to thank-you.
