## Why this matters

You have a question about SideKick PS. You want an answer now — in plain
English, not a technical manual. You already use ChatGPT or Claude for other
things. Now you can use them as your personal SideKick PS expert.

## How it works

1. Open your browser and go to **ps.ghl-sidekick.com/help.html**.
2. Click the orange **Copy SideKick PS Help Text** button.
3. Open any AI chat — ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini (free accounts work).
4. Paste the text into the chat.
5. Ask your question in your own words.

Example questions you can ask:

- "How do I show a QR code on the screen?"
- "What do I do if the GoCardless mandate is not found?"
- "How do I print a PDF of the order?"
- "What does the Camera button do?"
- "How do I change the toolbar colour?"

The AI reads the entire manual and answers based on it. You can keep asking
follow-up questions in the same conversation — it remembers the context.

## Why this works

The help text contains the complete SideKick PS knowledge base — every
feature, every setting, every common problem and fix. When you paste it into
the AI, you are giving it the instruction manual. The AI becomes your
personal SideKick PS expert.

## Bookmark it

The help page is updated whenever SideKick PS gets new features. Bookmark
**ps.ghl-sidekick.com/help.html** and return whenever you need fresh help
text.

## From inside SideKick PS

Open **Settings → About** and click the **🤖 AI Help (ChatGPT)** link. It
opens the help page directly in your browser.
