## Why this matters

A client just arrived for their viewing. They are excited, emotional, and
ready to buy. Your job is to guide them through the images, build their order,
lock in the payment, and send them home happy — without touching a spreadsheet
or typing a single client detail twice. This chapter follows one sale from
hello to thank-you card.

## Before you start

- SideKick PS is installed, activated, and connected to GoHighLevel.
- ProSelect is open with the client's album loaded.
- The client's contact record exists in GoHighLevel.

## The session

### 1. Load the client

Click the **Get Client** button on the toolbar (the person+ icon, blue), or
press **Ctrl+Shift+G**. SideKick PS reads the album name, searches GoHighLevel
for the matching client, and loads their name, email, and contact ID. You will
see a confirmation with their name — click **Yes** to confirm.

If multiple matches appear, pick the correct one from the list.

### 2. Present the images

This is all you. Do what you do best — show the photographs, tell the stories,
let the client fall in love with their images. SideKick PS stays quiet while
you work.

### 3. Build the order

As the client chooses their favourites, build the order in ProSelect as you
normally would. When you are ready to take payment, open **Add Payment** in
ProSelect. A **📅 PayPlan** button appears in the payment window — SideKick PS
put it there.

### 4. Take the deposit and set up the payment plan

Click **📅 PayPlan** (or press **Ctrl+Shift+P**) to open the Payment
Calculator. Here is what you do:

- Confirm the **Balance Due** is correct.
- Enter a deposit amount (or leave it blank if no deposit).
- Set **No. Payments** — how many monthly payments after the deposit.
- Choose a **Pay Type** — usually **GoCardless DD** for automated collection.
- Pick a **Recurring** frequency — **Monthly** is most common.
- Ask the client which day of the month suits them — select it under
  **Start Date**.
- Click **✓ Schedule Payments**.

SideKick PS enters every payment line into ProSelect automatically. No typing
dates and amounts one by one.

### 5. If using GoCardless — set up the mandate

If the client needs a Direct Debit mandate, this is the moment. Click the
**QR Code** button on the toolbar. A full-screen display appears on your
presentation monitor.

- **Slide 1** shows your studio Wi-Fi — the client scans it and joins your
  network.
- Press **↓** to move to **Slide 2** — your GoCardless mandate invitation.
  The client scans it and fills in their bank details on their own phone.
- While they do that, continue with the payment plan in ProSelect.

When the mandate is complete, click the **GoCardless** button on the toolbar.
SideKick PS finds the mandate automatically using the client's name and
email, and links the payment schedule to it.

Press **Escape** or click the **QR Code** button again to close the display.

### 6. Sync the invoice

Once the order is finalised in ProSelect, click the **Sync Invoice** button
(the document icon, green) on the toolbar. SideKick PS exports the order and
creates the invoice in GoHighLevel — complete with every line item, payment
schedule, and a contact sheet image.

If an invoice already exists for this shoot, SideKick PS asks what you want
to do: **Replace** it, **Update** it, or create a **New** one alongside. If
previous payments exist, **Update** is the safe choice — it preserves those
payments.

### 7. Send a thank-you card

Click the **Cardly** button on the toolbar. A preview appears showing a
printed postcard with the client's name and address, ready to send. Review
it and click **Send**. The postcard lands on their doormat within a few
working days — a physical reminder of how much they loved their images.

### What success looks like

The client leaves. The payment plan is scheduled. The invoice is in
GoHighLevel. A printed thank-you card is on its way. You close ProSelect
and go home — no admin to catch up on.

## When things go wrong

- **"No client found"** — The album name may not match the GoHighLevel
  contact record closely enough. Open GHL in your browser, search for the
  client manually, and use **Use Other** in the lookup dialog to select them.
- **Mandate not found** — SideKick PS tries email first, then name. If both
  fail, a **Use Other** option lets you search by a different name or email
  (for example, if a partner is paying).
- **Duplicate invoice warning** — This is normal if you have synced this
  shoot before. Choose **Update** to keep existing payments, or **Replace**
  to start fresh.
