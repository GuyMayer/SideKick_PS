## Why this matters

The toolbar is SideKick PS. Every button does one job — and that job replaces
something you used to do by hand. This page explains every button in plain
English so you know exactly what to click and when.

## GHL section (left)

| Icon | Button | What it does | Best time to use it |
|------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| 👤+ | **Get Client** | Searches GoHighLevel for the client matching the open album and loads their name, email, and contact ID | At the start of every viewing session |
| 📄 | **Sync Invoice** | Exports the ProSelect order to GoHighLevel as an invoice with line items, payments, and a contact sheet | Immediately after finalising the order |
| 🏭 | **Production** | Opens the production pipeline record for this shoot in GoHighLevel | When you need to check lab progress or order status (only appears after syncing) |
| 🪪 | **Open GHL Contact** | Opens the client's full GoHighLevel contact page in your browser | When you need to check notes, send a message, or review history mid-session |

## Shortcuts section (middle)

| Icon | Button | What it does | Best time to use it |
|------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| 🧾 | **Review Order** | Opens the Review Order screen in ProSelect for the current album | When you need to check or adjust the full order before printing or syncing |
| 📷 | **Camera** | Captures a photo of the room or wall display and saves it to your room capture folder | After arranging a wall grouping — capture it for the room view or to email the client |
| 📂 | **Open Folder** | Opens the current shoot's image folder in your file browser (Explorer, Bridge, or Lightroom — you choose in Settings) | When you need to access raw files without navigating through folders |
| Ps | **Photoshop** | Sends the selected image to Photoshop for editing and refreshes ProSelect when you save | When a client asks "can you remove that?" — quick edit and back |
| LB | **Light Blue** | Opens the current client's record in Light Blue (photo business management software) | If you use Light Blue for client and order management alongside ProSelect |
| 🔄 | **Refresh** | Reloads the album's images from disk | After adding, renaming, or editing images outside ProSelect |
| 🔤 | **Sort** | Toggles between random and alphabetical image order in the album | During a sales session when you want to control the presentation sequence |
| 🖨 | **Quick Print** | Sends the current ProSelect page straight to your selected printer — no dialog | When a client wants a printed copy right now |
| 📕 | **PDF** | Prints the current page to a PDF file automatically | When you need a digital copy of the order or a page to email |
| ✉ | **Email PDF** | Prints the page to PDF and emails it to the client via GoHighLevel in one click | After the sale — send a copy of the order summary while they are still excited |
| 🔲 | **QR Code** | Shows a full-screen display on your presentation monitor — QR codes for Wi-Fi, GoCardless mandates, or bank transfer details | During payment setup — the client scans from their phone; no peering at your screen |
| ⬇ | **Download** | Copies images from a camera SD card to your shoot archive | At the start of your workflow — get images off the card and into the archive |

## Services section (right)

| Icon | Button | What it does | Best time to use it |
|------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| GC | **GoCardless** | Checks for an existing Direct Debit mandate or sends a new mandate request — then links payments to the mandate | Right after scheduling a payment plan with GoCardless DD |
| ✉ | **Cardly** | Opens a postcard preview with the client's name and address, ready to send a printed thank-you card | At the end of the sale — the card arrives when the excitement is still fresh |
| ⚙ | **Settings** | Opens the SideKick PS settings panel | When you need to adjust anything — GHL connection, toolbar look, hotkeys, display content |
