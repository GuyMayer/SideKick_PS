## Why this matters

You have just installed SideKick PS. You want it working before your next
client walks through the door. This chapter gets you there — licence
activated, GoHighLevel connected, toolbar visible. Ten minutes, then you are
ready for real work.

## Before you start

- You need the SideKick PS installer (from your purchase email or the website).
- You need your GoHighLevel login details.
- ProSelect should be installed on the same computer.

## Install

Double-click the installer and follow the prompts. SideKick PS starts
automatically. You will see its icon appear in the Windows taskbar tray — a
little rocket. The floating toolbar also appears alongside ProSelect if
ProSelect is open.

## Activate your licence

1. Click the **Settings** gear icon on the toolbar (or press **Ctrl+Shift+I**).
2. Go to the **About** tab on the left.
3. Click **Enter License Key**.
4. Paste the licence key from your purchase confirmation email.
5. Click **Activate**.

The About tab now shows your name and **Active** status. All features are
unlocked.

## Connect to GoHighLevel

SideKick PS needs permission to talk to your GoHighLevel account.

1. In GoHighLevel (your browser), go to **Settings → Integrations → Private Integrations**.
2. Create a new **Private Integration** token and copy it.
3. In GoHighLevel, go to **Settings → Business Profile** and copy your **Location ID**.
4. Back in SideKick PS, open **Settings → GHL Integration**.
5. Paste the API key and Location ID into the fields.
6. Click **Save & Test**.

A green confirmation appears when the connection is working. The toolbar's
**Get Client**, **Sync Invoice**, and **Open GHL Contact** buttons are now
active.

## Check the toolbar

Look at the toolbar docked to the top-right of your ProSelect window. If
ProSelect is not open yet, open any album — the toolbar appears.

The toolbar follows ProSelect automatically. If it is not where you want it,
**Ctrl+Click** the dotted grab handle (⣿) on the left edge and drag it to a
new position.

## What success looks like

ProSelect is open with an album. The toolbar is visible and docked to
ProSelect. Every button on it works — you can click **Get Client** and it
searches GoHighLevel. You are ready for your first sale.

## When things go wrong

- **"License invalid"** — You may have copied extra spaces around the key.
  Keys are case-sensitive. Copy the key again directly from the email.
- **GHL connection fails** — Make sure you are using a V2 Private Integration
  Token, not an older API key. Generate a new one in GHL if unsure.
- **Toolbar not appearing** — Right-click the SideKick PS tray icon and select
  **Reload**. The toolbar reappears at its last saved position.
