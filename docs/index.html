Frequently Asked Questions

Which versions of ProSelect does it support?
ProSelect 2022 and 2025 are both supported. SideKick auto-detects which version you're running. Currently Windows only (Windows 10/11).
Do I need a GoHighLevel account?
For invoice sync and client import, yes — you need a GHL account with API access. The payment plan calculator and room capture work without GHL.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. All data flows directly between your computer and GoHighLevel's API. Nothing is stored on our servers. Your API keys are encrypted and saved locally on your machine only.
What if I switch computers?
Export your settings from the old computer and import them on the new one. Your license is tied to your GHL Location ID, not a specific machine — just deactivate on the old computer and activate on the new one.
Does it work with GoCardless / Direct Debit?
Yes. GoCardless Direct Debit is fully integrated and works internationally — UK, USA, EU, Australia, Canada & New Zealand. The payment calculator automatically enforces the required setup window for each region. You can mix payment methods — for example, a credit card deposit with DD recurring payments.
How does the free trial work?
Start your trial through LemonSqueezy — enter your payment details and you'll get 14 days completely free with every feature unlocked. If it's not for you, cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged a penny. If you love it, your subscription continues automatically at £14.99/month (billed annually). You can cancel at any time from your LemonSqueezy account.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel your subscription at any time through your LemonSqueezy account. Your license remains active until the end of the current billing period.
What if I need to change an order after syncing?
Just re-export from ProSelect. SideKick detects the existing invoice and asks whether to Update (change items in-place, preserve payments), Replace (delete old, create new), or create a New separate invoice. Existing GoCardless and Stripe payments are never affected.
