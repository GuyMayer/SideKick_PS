## Welcome to SideKick PS

You became a photographer to create beautiful images — not to spend your evenings typing invoices, chasing missed payments, or copying client details between programmes. SideKick PS handles all of that for you. It sits quietly alongside ProSelect, and with one click on the toolbar it syncs your order to GoHighLevel, builds a payment plan, sets up a Direct Debit, or sends a printed thank-you card to your client's doormat.

By the end of today, you will have:

- Fewer clients who "forget" to pay
- Invoices that create themselves
- A thank-you card landing on their mat before the excitement fades
- Your evenings back

SideKick PS is a floating toolbar that docks to your ProSelect window. Each button does one thing — and each thing saves you about five minutes of typing per client.

| Section | What it handles |
|---------|----------------|
| **GHL** (left) | Look up clients, sync invoices, open their GoHighLevel record |
| **Shortcuts** (middle) | Review orders, capture room photos, refresh albums, quick print, PDFs, QR codes |
| **Services** (right) | GoCardless Direct Debit mandates, Cardly printed postcards |

**How this manual works:** Start with the Quick Start Guide — you will be up and running in 10 minutes. "Your First Sale" walks you through a complete client session. The Toolbar Reference explains every button. The Troubleshooting chapter covers the most common problems.

## Get Running in 10 Minutes

You have just installed SideKick PS. You want it working before your next client walks through the door. Ten minutes, then you are ready for real work.

### Before you start

- You need the SideKick PS installer (from your purchase email or the website).
- You need your GoHighLevel login details.
- ProSelect should be installed on the same computer.

### Install

Double-click the installer and follow the prompts. SideKick PS starts automatically. You will see its icon appear in the Windows taskbar tray — a little rocket. The floating toolbar also appears alongside ProSelect if ProSelect is open.

### Activate your licence

1. Click the **Settings** gear icon on the toolbar (or press **Ctrl+Shift+I**).
2. Go to the **About** tab on the left.
3. Click **Enter License Key**.
4. Paste the licence key from your purchase confirmation email.
5. Click **Activate**.

The About tab now shows your name and **Active** status. All features are unlocked.

### Connect to GoHighLevel

1. In GoHighLevel (your browser), go to **Settings → Integrations → Private Integrations**.
2. Create a new Private Integration token and copy it.
3. In GoHighLevel, go to **Settings → Business Profile** and copy your Location ID.
4. Back in SideKick PS, open **Settings → GHL Integration**.
5. Paste the API key and Location ID into the fields.
6. Click **Save & Test**.

A green confirmation appears when the connection is working. The toolbar's **Get Client**, **Sync Invoice**, and **Open GHL Contact** buttons are now active.

### Check the toolbar

Open any album in ProSelect. The toolbar appears docked to the top-right of the window. If it is not where you want it, **Ctrl+Click** the dotted grab handle (⣿) on the left edge and drag it.

### What success looks like

ProSelect is open with an album. The toolbar is visible. Every button works. You are ready for your first sale.

### When things go wrong

- **"License invalid"** — Check that you copied the full key with no extra spaces. Keys are case-sensitive.
- **GHL connection fails** — Make sure you are using a V2 Private Integration Token. Generate a new one in GHL if unsure.
- **Toolbar not appearing** — Right-click the SideKick PS tray icon and select **Reload**.

## Your First Sale — Start to Finish

A client just arrived for their viewing. They are excited, emotional, and ready to buy. Your job is to guide them through the images, build their order, lock in the payment, and send them home happy — without touching a spreadsheet.

### Before you start

- SideKick PS is installed, activated, and connected to GoHighLevel.
- ProSelect is open with the client's album loaded.
- The client's contact record exists in GoHighLevel.

### 1. Load the client

Click the **Get Client** button on the toolbar (the person+ icon, blue), or press **Ctrl+Shift+G**. SideKick PS reads the album name, searches GoHighLevel for the matching client, and loads their name, email, and contact ID. Confirm with **Yes**.

### 2. Present the images

This is all you. Do what you do best — show the photographs, tell the stories. SideKick PS stays quiet.

### 3. Build the order

Build the order in ProSelect as you normally would. When ready to take payment, open **Add Payment**. A **📅 PayPlan** button appears — SideKick PS put it there.

### 4. Take the deposit and set up the payment plan

Click **📅 PayPlan** (or press **Ctrl+Shift+P**). In the Payment Calculator:

- Confirm the **Balance Due** is correct.
- Enter a deposit amount (or leave blank).
- Set **No. Payments**, choose **GoCardless DD** as the Pay Type, pick **Monthly**.
- Ask the client which day of the month suits them — select it under **Start Date**.
- Click **✓ Schedule Payments**.

Every payment line is entered into ProSelect automatically. No typing dates and amounts one by one.

### 5. If using GoCardless — set up the mandate

Click the **QR Code** button. A full-screen display appears on your presentation monitor. Slide 1 shows your studio Wi-Fi — the client scans and joins your network. Press **↓** for Slide 2 — your GoCardless mandate invitation. The client fills in their bank details on their own phone.

When ready, click the **GoCardless** button on the toolbar. SideKick PS finds the mandate and links the schedule. Press **Escape** to close the display.

### 6. Sync the invoice

Click the **Sync Invoice** button (green document icon). The order is exported and the invoice created in GoHighLevel. If a duplicate warning appears, choose **Update** to keep existing payments.

### 7. Send a thank-you card

Click the **Cardly** button. Review the preview and click **Send**. A printed postcard arrives at their home within days.

### What success looks like

The client leaves. The payment plan is scheduled. The invoice is in GoHighLevel. A thank-you card is on its way. You go home — no admin.

## Payment Plans — Lock In the Sale

A client says yes to a £2,000 wall grouping. If they walk out without committing to a payment schedule, doubt creeps in overnight. The Payment Calculator lets you build the full plan in seconds.

### Before you start

- ProSelect must be open with the client's order ready.
- The **Add Payment** dialog must be open in ProSelect.

### Steps

1. Open **Add Payment** in ProSelect. SideKick PS places a **📅 PayPlan** button inside the window.
2. Click **📅 PayPlan** or press **Ctrl+Shift+P** to open the Payment Calculator.
3. Confirm the **Balance Due** is correct. Close and fix the order in ProSelect if not.
4. Enter a deposit amount and payment method (or leave blank for no deposit).
5. Set **No. Payments**, choose a **Pay Type** (GoCardless DD for automated collection), select a **Recurring** frequency (Monthly is most common).
6. Ask the client which day works best — select it under **Start Date**.
7. Click **✓ Schedule Payments**. Every line is entered into ProSelect automatically.

### The rounding setting

Sometimes a balance does not divide perfectly. Under the deposit section, **Add rounding to:** lets you choose whether the difference goes into the deposit or the first payment. Your choice is remembered.

### What success looks like

All payment lines appear in ProSelect with correct amounts, dates, and types. Done.

## GoCardless — Get Paid Without Chasing

Chasing late payments is the worst part of running a photography business. GoCardless Direct Debit means the money comes automatically. No reminders, no awkward texts.

### During a sale

1. Click the **QR Code** button — Slide 1 shows your Wi-Fi QR. Client scans and joins.
2. Press **↓** for Slide 2 — your GoCardless mandate invitation. Client fills in bank details on their phone.
3. While they do that, build the payment plan in ProSelect. Choose **GoCardless DD** as the Pay Type.
4. After scheduling payments and completing the order, come out of Review Order. SideKick PS prompts: "Now press the GoCardless button."
5. Click the **GoCardless** button. SideKick PS finds the mandate and links the schedule.

### Use Another — when someone else is paying

If the payer is not the client in the album (a parent, partner), click **Use Another** and type their name or email.

### What success looks like

The GoCardless dashboard shows the mandate active with the schedule attached. You receive money on the scheduled dates.

### When things go wrong

- **Mandate not found** — Tries email, then name. If both fail, send a setup request.
- **GoCardless button missing** — Enable in **Settings → GoCardless**.

## After They Leave — Invoice, PDF, Postcard

The client has left. Here is everything that happens next.

### Sync the invoice

Click the **Sync Invoice** button (green document icon). The order is exported and the invoice created in GoHighLevel. If a duplicate exists, choose **Update** (keeps payments) or **Replace** (start fresh).

### Print a PDF

Click the **PDF** button (red document icon). The page is printed to PDF automatically. First use: calibrate in **Settings → Toolbar → Calibrate Print Button**.

### Email the PDF

Click the **Email PDF** button (envelope icon). Prints to PDF and emails it via GoHighLevel. Choose your email template in **Settings → GHL Integration → PDF Email Template**.

### Send a printed thank-you card

Click the **Cardly** button. Review the preview and click **Send**. A printed postcard arrives within days.

### Capture a room photo

Click the **Camera** button (red/maroon). SideKick PS captures the ProSelect room view as a JPG. Click **Email** to send it to the client.

### What success looks like

Invoice in GoHighLevel. PDF in the client's inbox. Postcard on its way. You are done for the day.

## The Display — QR Codes, Bank Details & Images

Your client should never squint at your laptop screen. The full-screen display puts QR codes and bank details on your big presentation monitor.

### What you can display

- **QR codes** — Wi-Fi credentials, GoCardless mandate links, review pages
- **Bank transfer details** — bank name, account holder, sort code, account number
- **Custom images** — your logo, welcome slide, seasonal offers

### Using the display

1. Click the **QR Code** button (grid icon, teal).
2. Display appears full-screen on your presentation monitor.
3. **↑ / ↓** arrow keys cycle through slides.
4. **← / →** arrow keys move between monitors.
5. **Escape** or click the QR Code button again to close.

### Setting up slides

Go to **Settings → Display**. Enter URLs or text in Slides 1–3. Fill in bank details at the bottom. Choose image files for custom slides.

### Sizing

- **Display Size** — QR code size as percentage of screen. Increase if clients have trouble scanning.
- **Bank Scale** — font size for bank details.
- **Monitor** — which screen the display appears on by default.

### When things go wrong

- **Blank slide** — No content set for that slide. Add a URL or text in Settings.
- **Hard to scan** — Increase Display Size in Settings.
- **Wrong monitor** — Use ←/→ arrow keys to move it.

## What this does

Links SideKick PS to your GoHighLevel account so it can look up clients, sync invoices, and push payment plans.

## Before you start

- You need a GoHighLevel account with a sub-account (Location) set up for your studio.
- You need a **Private Integration Token** (API key) from your GHL sub-account — found in **Settings → Integrations → Private Integrations** inside GHL.

## What you should see

The GHL tab in Settings shows a green confirmation. The toolbar's **Get Client**, **Sync Invoice**, and **Open GHL Contact** buttons become fully active.

- **"Connection failed"** — Check that your API key is a V2 Private Integration Token, not an older API key. Generate a new one in GHL under **Settings → Integrations → Private Integrations**.
- **"Location ID not found"** — Make sure you are using the Location ID, not the Agency ID. These are different values found in different parts of GHL settings.

## What this does

Changes the toolbar's size, icon colour, and screen position so it sits naturally alongside your ProSelect window.

## What this does

Calculates and schedules all payment lines for a client's order directly into ProSelect in one step — splitting the balance into a deposit and regular payments so you never have to enter them manually.

## Before you start

- ProSelect's **Add Payment** dialog must be open for the current order.
- SideKick PS must be running.

## Steps

1. In ProSelect, open the **Add Payment** dialog. A **📅 PayPlan** button appears automatically in the dialog.
2. Click **📅 PayPlan** — or press **Ctrl+Shift+P** from anywhere — to open the Payment Calculator.
3. Confirm the **Balance Due** shown at the top is correct. If not, close the calculator, correct the order in ProSelect, then reopen it.
4. In the **Downpayment / Deposit** section, enter the deposit amount and select the payment method. The date defaults to today — change it if needed.
5. In the **Scheduled Payments** section, set **No. Payments**, choose a **Pay Type**, and select a **Recurring** frequency (Monthly, Weekly, Bi-Weekly, or 4-Weekly).
6. Ask the client which day they prefer for payments, then select it under **Start Date** along with the start month.
7. Click **✓ Schedule Payments** — SideKick PS enters all payment lines into ProSelect automatically.

## What you should see

Each payment line appears in the ProSelect Add Payment list with the correct amount, date, and payment type — ready to save.

## Something went wrong?

- **Balance Due shows the wrong amount** — Close the calculator, correct the order total in ProSelect, then reopen via **📅 PayPlan**.
- **"GoCardless DD" start date was adjusted automatically** — Direct Debit mandates require a minimum setup period. SideKick PS has moved the start date to the earliest valid date to comply with GoCardless requirements.

## What this does

Controls where the rounding difference goes when a balance cannot be split into perfectly equal payments — either added to the deposit or to the first scheduled payment.

## Before you start

- The Payment Calculator must be open (see How to build a payment plan with the Payment Calculator).

## Steps

1. Open the Payment Calculator (**📅 PayPlan** button in the Add Payment dialog, or **Ctrl+Shift+P**).
2. In the **Downpayment / Deposit** section, find the **Add rounding to:** radio buttons.
3. Select **Downpayment** to add the rounding difference to the deposit amount, or **1st Payment** to add it to the first scheduled payment.

_Example: a £208.33 balance split 3 ways gives £69.44 × 3 = £208.32, leaving a 1p rounding difference. This setting controls where that 1p goes._

## What you should see

A rounding info line below the deposit amount updates to show the adjustment amount (e.g. "Rounding of £0.01 added to deposit"). Your preference is saved for future sessions.

## Something went wrong?

- **No rounding notice appears** — The balance divides exactly, so no adjustment is needed and the notice will not show.

- Two QR codes must be set up in **Settings → Display**:
  - **Slide 1** — your studio Wi-Fi credentials
  - **Slide 2** — your GoCardless mandate invitation link
- The client's contact record must already be loaded in SideKick PS
  (use **Get Client** first if not).

## Steps

1. Click the **QR Code** button on the SideKick toolbar — the display appears full-screen
   on your presentation monitor showing Slide 1 (Wi-Fi). Use the **↑ / ↓ arrow keys** to
   move between the three available slides. Use the **← / → arrow keys** to move the
   display between monitors if you have more than one screen.

2. Ask the client to scan the Wi-Fi QR code with their phone and join your studio network.
   Confirm they are connected before continuing.

3. Press **↓** to move to Slide 2 (GoCardless mandate invitation). The client scans it and
   begins filling in their bank details on their phone.

4. While the client completes the mandate form on their phone, open **Add Payment** in
   ProSelect and click **📅 PayPlan** (or press **Ctrl+Shift+P**) to open the Payment
   Calculator.

5. Confirm the **Balance Due** is correct. Enter a downpayment amount and payment method
   if applicable.

6. Set **No. Payments**, select **GoCardless DD** as the Pay Type, choose a **Recurring**
   frequency (Monthly or 4-Weekly), then ask the client which day of the month works best
   for them and select it under **Start Date**.

7. Click **✓ Schedule Payments** — SideKick PS enters all payment lines into ProSelect
   automatically.

8. Complete the order in ProSelect and come out of the Review Order section. SideKick PS
   displays a prompt: _"The pay plan has been documented. Now press the GoCardless button
   on the toolbar to set it up."_

9. Press **Escape** or click the **QR Code** button again to dismiss the display, then click
   the **GoCardless** button on the toolbar. SideKick PS automatically locates the client's
   mandate using their name and email address and links the payment schedule to it — no
   manual lookup required.

## What you should see

All payment lines appear in ProSelect's payment list with the correct amounts and dates.
The GoCardless dashboard shows the mandate as active with the schedule attached.

## Something went wrong?

- **Mandate not found by email** — SideKick PS will automatically retry using the client's
  name. If it still cannot match, a **Use Other** option appears — use this if a family
  member or different person is paying under a different name or email address.
- **Client cannot scan the QR code** — Increase the display size in **Settings → Display**
  or use the arrow keys to move the display to the correct monitor.
- **GoCardless button is missing from the toolbar** — GoCardless must be enabled in
  **Settings → GoCardless** before the button appears.
